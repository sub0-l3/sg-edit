When I woke up in the morning, the sun was shining again. I looked around me, wondering where I was. And then I remembered. I was a baby sea turtle - an olive ridley - in the middle of a big blue world, which I had heard someone call the ocean. There was blue and green water in every direction, and the sunlight was playing games with the clouds. I tucked my flippers beneath my body and floated for a while, "PLEEEEEAASE??" letting the current carry me.


I did not know where I was. But that does not matter when the sun is shining, and your stomach is full.

The sun warmed me, and filled me with new energy.
